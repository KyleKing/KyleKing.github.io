From Prototyping to Compiling

February 2, 2014

Over winter break and through the beggining of the spring, I have been trying to learn how to edit HTML, CSS, and Javascript, in hopes of building a responsive and interactive website. Today, I get to say that I finally have achieved my goal from just two months ago. This site is the most advanced of all of my mockups and designs and is finally ready for release.

Much of my initial inspiration came from Google Ventures. Like all things Google, they played around and ended up with something awesome. Also checkout CoDrops if you aren't familiar with their experimental design. They do some really interesting and beautiful stuff.
