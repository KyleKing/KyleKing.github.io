With 4K for Cancer, every donation makes an impact.

 

The Giver ($10+)
Every donation makes a difference
The Life-Changer ($25+)
$25 will pay for transporting a patient to a doctor’s appointment
The Shining Light ($50+)
$50 will pay to keep a patient’s lights and power on during treatment
The Facilitator ($75+)
$75 will pay for three hours of UCF Navigator’s time working with patients and families
The Supporter ($100+)
$100 will pay for UCF patient navigators to facilitate a monthly support group
The Life-Saver ($200+)
$200 will pay to help preserve a young adult’s fertility prior to starting treatment
The Navigator ($500+)
$500 will pay for UCF Navigator to meet and work with five new patients and their families
The Guide ($1,000+)
$1,000 will pay for gas cards to help 40 patients get to treatment

All donations are tax deductible to the extent allowed by law. 4k for Cancer is a 501(c) Non-Profit Company (EIN: 52-2057636).

Donor Benefits

Individual Donations
Our Sincerest Gratitude:

Because of donors like you, the Ulman Cancer Fund and the 4K for Cancer are able to continue their efforts in providing the guidance that young adults need.

Each donor who makes a donation within one of the brackets above will be listed on this website in recognition of their donation. If you do not wish to be listed, simply make a donation as normal, but make sure to select "NO" for the "Keep Anonymous" box.

Dedication Days:

Each day on the ride, I will be dedicating the ride to loved ones who have been affected by cancer. With a donation of $50 dollars, I will dedicate a day to your loved one.

In previous years, 4K riders have written on the back of their calfs to show who they are dedicating that day's ride to. Additionally, I can post pictures and may feature a story in dedication to someone whose life has been effected cancer.

Corporate/Matching Donations
Image and Blog Recognition:

For each company that donates over $25, including any donation made as an employee matching donation, I will thank the company on my website, Facebook page, and on my 4K official blog. Along with a second post in all three places further along in the summer.

Internet Logo Placement:

For any donation of over $50, I will place the company's logo along the bottom of each page of this website, along with blog posts multiple times during the summer ride.

Summer-Ride Logo Placement:

Along the ride, I will be taking pictures at 4K events and in front of picturesque surroundings. To show gratitude for the company's that have gone above and beyond in donations of over $100, I will feature their logo on the back of a custom t-shirt that I will wear along the route.
