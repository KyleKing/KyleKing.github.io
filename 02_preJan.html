Making a Website

December 1, 2013

The first version of the website was built entirely in Azure to get a working prototype before I delve into learning and editing code. Let me know what you think!

I hope to use the site as an easy way to convey up to date news on fundraising events, my progress both before and during the ride, and the wonderful people and companies that make this ride possible.

Edit: I finally made this site with code, to view the older version of this website, visit: http://stable.bikingagainstcancer.com/

 

Race Pace Bicycles

December 31, 2013

Big shout out to Race Pace Bicycles! They are providing the bikes for us to ride this summer and are a big reason that a ride like this is possible. I can't wait to get out and ride mine!

 

Posters

January 23, 2014

Over winter break I spent three exciting weeks working in the world of ocean carbon (OC) management at Sustainametrix. It was exciting to be working from the perspective of a relative outsider to the OC community, while attempting to understand the history and accomplishments of the very active community. Upon leaving to return to school, I left one of my very awesome poster on the door!
